The date, as well as the place of his birth, is uncertain, but it is probable that he was born in , the year after his father’s accession to the mayoralty, and was therefore under twelve years of age when he was sent by his father to accompany Pope Stephen II. on his journey of not less than miles from St. Maurice in Switzerland, to Ponthieu in Champagne.
